# about

About me

I'm a data scientist with a passion for turning complex data into actionable insights that drive business impact. With a solid foundation in both technical and business disciplines, I’ve designed and deployed end-to-end data solutions—from integrating multi-source datasets and building predictive models to developing interactive dashboards that support real-time decision-making. turning complex problems into simple, beautiful and intuitive designs.

My background spans roles in consulting, and data science, I spearheaded data modeling and engineering initiatives that streamlined processes for 170,000 employees and enabled seamless model deployment. My experience further extends to building and A/B testing a collaborative filtering recommendation system at Fashom I thrive on solving challenging problems with innovative techniques and continually strive to expand my expertise across the entire data workflow, ensuring that every insight I uncover not only informs strategy but also creates tangible value.
